About Ayurveda

The word Ayurveda is derived from Sanskrit and means “science of life”. Ayurveda is one of the world’s oldest medical systems, originated in India over 5000 years ago. It relies on a natural and holistic approach to physical, mental, and emotional health. “Prayojanam chasya swasthasya swasthya rakshanam aturasya vikara prashamanam cha” (The aim of Ayurveda is to protect the health of the healthy & to alleviate disorders in the diseased.)

Our Services

Shirodhara

A traditional therapy where a stream of medicated oil is poured on the forehead. It helps relieve stress, insomnia, anxiety, depression, and improves mental clarity.

Janubasti

A warm medicated oil therapy focused on knee joints, effective in osteoarthritis, ligament injuries, joint stiffness and degeneration-related pain.

Katibasti

Panchakarma Therapy

Panchakarma is the sodhana chikitsa, a special Ayurvedic treatment which not only eliminate toxins from the body but also nourish and rejuvenate the body . It also helps to balance the dosas and restore the good health.
